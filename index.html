FAQ

Questions, answered

Everything you need to know before your first session.

Do I need my own pool?
Yes, sessions take place in your home or community pool. If you don't have access to one, let us know and we can suggest partner venues nearby.
What ages do you coach?
We coach children from age 3 through adults of any age and skill level, including complete beginners.
Can I request a female coach?
Absolutely — select "Female Coaches" when booking, or mention it via WhatsApp, and we'll match you accordingly.
What if I need to reschedule?
You can reschedule any session up to 12 hours in advance at no charge, directly through WhatsApp.
Are coaches insured and background-checked?
Every coach on CoachNow completes identity verification, a background check, and holds a valid swim instruction certification before joining.
